HirePilot

Your Personal AI Career Operating System

HirePilot is a local-first, privacy-respecting career OS that automates job matching, resume tailoring, and interview prep on your own machine.

View on GitHub Documentation

What It Does

📄

Resume Intelligence

Parses uploaded resume files and extracts skills, profiles, and evidence nodes.

🕸️

Career Knowledge Graph

Constructs and relates skills and projects in a local PostgreSQL instance.

🔍

AI Job Discovery

Scrapes and filters roles across 13+ sources based on candidate preferences.

💬

Interview Preparation

Generates tailored STAR talking points and role-specific mock questions.
